# WORLD

STATUS: WORLD
SCOPE: UNIVERSAL

## Purpose

Defines world-side existence during prolonged future warfare.

This layer controls:

- frontline civilization structure
- military-industrial infrastructure
- wartime environmental condition
- continuous survival atmosphere

This layer defines WHAT KIND OF WORLD exists.

This layer must remain independent from:

- camera interpretation
- rendering philosophy
- character identity

---

# WORLD STRUCTURE

## [CivilizationStructure]

continental-scale frontline logistics civilization,

city functions merged with military infrastructure,

civilian districts existing under permanent wartime administration,

supply-chain-centered urban planning,

high-density refugee migration zones,

industrial production operating twenty-four hours continuously,

automated defense systems integrated into public infrastructure,

partial collapse of national borders replaced by regional command sectors,

long-term war economy normalized into daily civilian life

Controls:

- civilization readability
- frontline survival identity
- wartime continuity

Must NOT define:

- character identity
- cinematic interpretation

---

## [ArchitectureLanguage]

massive reinforced concrete megastructures,

layered urban fortification belts,

elevated transport rails for armored logistics movement,

anti-air tower clusters integrated into residential districts,

modular emergency shelters embedded beneath public buildings,

industrial cooling pipes exposed across city exterior surfaces,

dense overhead utility wiring between structures,

vertical construction optimized for artillery resistance,

mixed military-civilian architecture without clear separation,

heavy use of armored shutters,
blast-resistant corridors,
maintenance catwalks,
external structural braces

Controls:

- spatial identity
- structural wartime readability
- environmental consistency

Must remain stable across:

- rendering variation
- cinematic variation

---

## [EnvironmentalCondition]

constant distant artillery vibration,

air partially contaminated by industrial smoke and fuel exhaust,

frequent ash rainfall from offshore bombardment zones,

weathered infrastructure with emergency repairs layered repeatedly over older damage,

visible concrete patchwork and steel reinforcement exposure,

persistent electrical instability in outer districts,

occasional siren broadcasts interrupting civilian activity,

supply shortages causing uneven lighting and intermittent power conservation,

cold wind tunnels formed between megastructures,

water drainage systems overloaded by chemical runoff and acid rain residue

Controls:

- environmental coherence
- wartime aging readability
- infrastructure exhaustion

Must NOT overwrite:

- architecture identity
- civilization identity

---

## [WorldDensity]

high civilian saturation near protected transit zones,

dense logistics movement at all hours,

military vehicles constantly passing through urban arteries,

crowded underground shelter markets,

tight industrial spacing with limited open sky visibility,

temporary structures expanding around permanent architecture,

maintenance drones and transport carriers continuously active,

small survival economies operating inside infrastructure gaps,

heavily layered environmental objects without collapsing spatial readability

Controls:

- environmental activity
- civilization pressure
- spatial breathing balance

---

## [AtmosphereBase]

persistent low-grade societal exhaustion,

normalized tension instead of active panic,

civilization surviving through routine rather than hope,

daily life continuing beneath distant warfare,

emotional numbness mixed with mechanical resilience,

public environments carrying constant background pressure,

temporary safety replacing true stability,

war no longer treated as an event,
but as climate

Controls:

- world-side atmosphere
- environmental emotional baseline
- civilization pressure tone

Must NOT define:

- camera emotion
- character psychology
