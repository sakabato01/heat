# WORLD

STATUS: WORLD  
SCOPE: UNIVERSAL

## Purpose

Defines dense layered metropolitan civilization under continuous civilian survival pressure.

This layer controls:

- vertical urban structure
- metropolitan expansion condition
- civilian continuity
- environmental atmosphere baseline

This layer defines WHAT KIND OF WORLD exists.

This layer must remain independent from:

- camera interpretation
- rendering philosophy
- character identity

---

# WORLD STRUCTURE

## [CivilizationStructure]

extremely dense metropolitan civilization,  
continuous infrastructure expansion,  
overloaded urban population systems,  
mixed regulated and unregulated city growth,  
aging but persistent civic operation

civilization developed through long-term spatial compression,  
resulting in layered urban accumulation instead of clean planning

public transportation remains highly functional,  
daily civilian routines continue despite environmental overcrowding

schools,  
small businesses,  
train systems,  
residential districts,  
street markets,  
convenience infrastructure,  
and maintenance services  
remain active throughout the city

civilization feels exhausted but stable

---

## [ArchitectureLanguage]

vertically compressed urban structures,  
interconnected residential-commercial buildings,  
narrow construction gaps,  
layered rooftop expansions,  
external piping systems,  
dense utility wiring,  
metal staircases attached across structures,  
stacked signage systems,  
small shrines and local storefronts embedded within urban density

architecture evolved incrementally over decades,  
creating uneven but functional environmental layering

large districts partially block natural light due to structural overgrowth

older concrete buildings coexist with newer infrastructure additions

the city prioritizes usable space over visual harmony

---

## [EnvironmentalCondition]

humid metropolitan atmosphere,  
heat accumulation between structures,  
reduced airflow in lower districts,  
slightly polluted urban air,  
water stains on aging surfaces,  
continuous infrastructure vibration,  
ventilation noise echoing through narrow streets

lower levels remain dim even during daytime due to vertical obstruction

environmental wear appears gradual and continuous,  
not catastrophic

air conditioning runoff,  
wet alley surfaces,  
rust accumulation,  
maintenance patchwork,  
and electrical noise  
remain common throughout the city

despite density and decay,  
the city remains continuously inhabited and operational

---

## [WorldDensity]

extremely high environmental density,  
constant civilian movement,  
compressed pedestrian pathways,  
crowded transit systems,  
small pockets of social breathing space embedded within dense districts

main roads remain highly active,  
while lower alleys become labyrinth-like and spatially compressed

upper commercial districts maintain brighter and more organized civilian flow

lower districts feel slower,  
heavier,  
and more infrastructurally saturated

the environment maintains readability despite structural overcrowding

---

## [AtmosphereBase]

urban exhaustion mixed with civilian persistence,  
quiet social loneliness within mass population density,  
warm artificial lighting against environmental fatigue,  
daily life continuing inside an overgrown city

the world feels crowded but alive

civilization appears continuously strained,  
yet emotionally resistant to collapse

ambient atmosphere remains grounded,  
human,  
and structurally oppressive without becoming dystopian spectacle

---

# RULES

## [SuppressionRules]

avoid explicit cyberpunk aesthetics,  
avoid futuristic holographic overload,  
avoid post-apocalyptic destruction,  
avoid pure noir criminal atmosphere,  
avoid exaggerated technological fantasy,  
avoid chaotic unreadable environmental clutter

---

## [Priority]

urban readability  
> vertical density coherence  
> civilian continuity  
> environmental layering  
> atmospheric pressure  
> micro detail
