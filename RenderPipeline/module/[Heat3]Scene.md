# Scene Language

STATUS: GLOBAL
SCOPE: UNIVERSAL

## Purpose

Defines environmental composition
and spatial framing language.

---

# SCENE

## [EnvironmentType]

industrial environments,
civilization-scale interiors,
urban peripheral structures,
large architectural spaces

---

## [SpatialComposition]

large negative space,
isolated character framing,
restrained environmental clutter,
readable silhouette spacing

---

## [EnvironmentalMood]

quiet atmosphere,
low social density,
industrial stillness,
restrained emotional tone

---

## [EnvironmentalCondition]

weathered structures,
soft environmental decay,
controlled spatial readability

---

# RULES

[SuppressionRules]

avoid dense environmental clutter,
avoid chaotic framing composition,
avoid excessive cinematic debris

[Priority]

environment supports silhouette
negative space > clutter
spatial readability > visual density
